Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long does it take to see results from digital marketing?

While some tactics like PPC advertising can show immediate results, most digital marketing strategies require 3-6 months to demonstrate significant ROI. SEO typically takes 4-6 months, while social media and content marketing show progressive improvements over time. We provide monthly reports so you can track your progress every step of the way.

What makes DigiBoost different from other agencies?

We combine data-driven strategies with creative excellence. Our team doesn't just execute campaigns ‚Äì we become an extension of your business. We focus on transparent reporting, personalized strategies, and measurable results. Plus, you get a dedicated account manager who understands your industry and goals.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?

We offer flexible engagement models. While we recommend at least a 6-month commitment to see substantial results, we also provide month-to-month options for businesses that prefer flexibility. Our goal is to earn your continued partnership through exceptional results, not contractual obligations.

Which digital marketing services do I need?

The right mix depends on your business goals, target audience, and industry. During our discovery phase, we'll conduct a comprehensive audit and recommend the optimal strategy. Most businesses benefit from a combination of SEO, content marketing, and paid advertising, but we'll customize our approach to your specific needs.

How do you measure success?

We establish clear KPIs aligned with your business objectives from day one. Common metrics include website traffic, conversion rates, lead generation, ROI, engagement rates, and revenue growth. You'll receive detailed monthly reports with actionable insights and strategic recommendations.

Can you work with my existing team?

Absolutely! We love collaborating with in-house teams. We can complement your existing marketing efforts, provide specialized expertise in specific areas, or take full ownership of your digital marketing. We're flexible and adapt to your preferred working style.
